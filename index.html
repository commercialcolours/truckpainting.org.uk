Truck painting in the UK

Finding the best company to paint your truck is essential when you wish your company to look at its best on the road. So, what must you look for when selecting a truck respray and paint refinishing service?

A professional truck painting service can paint trucks from all makes and models, from smaller trucks right up to the biggest commercial vehicles. Search for a truck painting company that paints new and old trucks, and one that offers a simple panel respray right up to full body overhauls.

 

Best truck painting services

The best truck painting services provide every type of paint finish and only use the best quality products, expertly matching paint manufacturers’ swatches using PPG and Glasurit computerised paint mixing systems. These will reproduce a precise colour match which—together with a dust-free environment—will produce a quality finish to match the most exacting standards.

Professional commercial truck painting services are EPA registered and have been approved by the Office of Fair Trading. They have the latest facilities and highly trained technicians.
